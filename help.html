Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find nearby resources?

Use the search bar on the home page to enter your location or click "Use Current Location" to automatically find resources near you. The map will display available shelters, food banks, medical centers, and other essential services.

What types of resources are available?

Our locator provides information on shelters, food distribution centers, medical facilities, water stations, supply depots, and emergency contacts. Resources are categorized by type for easy identification.

Is the information up-to-date?

We strive to keep our resource database current, but during active disasters, information can change rapidly. Always verify with local authorities and the resource providers directly for the most current status.

How can I volunteer?

Visit our Contact page to fill out the volunteer sign-up form. We welcome volunteers with various skills including logistics, medical support, counseling, and administrative assistance.

What should I do in an emergency?

In any emergency, call 911 immediately. Follow instructions from local authorities and emergency management agencies. Use our locator to find nearby emergency resources and shelters.

How do I report inaccurate information?

If you find outdated or incorrect information, please contact us through the Contact page. We appreciate your help in keeping our resource database accurate and reliable.
